Learn how to make the famous Chicken Binakol!

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, crushed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 large green papaya, peeled and sliced into strips
  • 1 cup malunggay (moringa) leaves (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  3. Add the coconut milk, chicken broth, lemongrass, and green papaya.
  4. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 30 minutes until the chicken is cooked and tender.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce to taste.
  6. Add the malunggay leaves last and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Binakol!
