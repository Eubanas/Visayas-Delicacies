Piaya Recipe

Make a Piaya in various step!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (or more as needed)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup muscovado sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, salt, water, and oil to form a dough. Knead until smooth.
  2. For the filling, mix muscovado sugar and a pinch of salt together.
  3. Roll the dough into small balls, flatten them, and place a small amount of the sugar filling in the center.
  4. Pinch the edges to seal and roll it out flat again.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and cook the piaya until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Optional: sprinkle with sesame seeds while cooking.
  7. Let the piaya cool before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Piaya!
