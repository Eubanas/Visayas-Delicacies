Humba Recipe

this various ingredients and instructions to make a humba!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks (with juice)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork belly pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the soy sauce, fish sauce, vinegar, sugar, pineapple with juice, and bay leaf. Stir to combine.
  4. Pour in the water and simmer for 40-60 minutes until the pork is tender and the sauce has reduced.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Humba!
