Chicken Molo Soup Recipe

Learn how to make the famous Chicken Molo Soup in Easy Step!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup minced shrimp (optional)
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 10 pieces wonton wrappers
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground chicken, minced shrimp, onions, fish sauce, cornstarch, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place a small spoonful of the filling in the center of each wonton wrapper, fold it, and seal the edges.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© the garlic, and add the chicken broth.
  4. Bring the broth to a boil, then drop the dumplings in and cook for 10-15 minutes or until the dumplings are tender.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Chicken Molo Soup!
