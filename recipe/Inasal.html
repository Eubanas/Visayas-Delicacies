Chicken Inasal Recipe

Learn how to make the famous Chicken Inasal, a delicious Filipino grilled chicken dish from Bacolod City!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger (minced)
  • 1/4 cup annatto oil (for basting)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine calamansi juice, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, lemongrass, ginger, salt, pepper, and sugar.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat a grill or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Thread the chicken pieces onto skewers (optional).
  5. Grill the chicken, basting occasionally with annatto oil, until cooked through and slightly charred on the outside. This should take about 20-30 minutes, depending on the size of the pieces.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice and dipping sauce of your choice (soy sauce with calamansi and chili is common).

Enjoy your delicious Chicken Inasal!
