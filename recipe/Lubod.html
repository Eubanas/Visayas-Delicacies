Lumpiang Ubod Recipe

Lumpiang Ubod is a Filipino fresh spring roll made with a delicious filling of sautéed vegetables and heart of palm (ubod).

Ingredients:

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups ubod (heart of palm), julienned
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup shrimps, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup pork, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

For the Wrapper:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch (diluted in 2 tbsp water)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

Prepare the Filling:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.
  2. Add pork and cook until browned. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  3. Stir in ubod and carrots. Season with soy sauce, fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until tender but not mushy. Set aside.

Make the Wrapper:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, water, eggs, salt, and oil until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over low heat. Pour a small amount of batter to make a thin crepe-like wrapper. Cook until set but not browned. Repeat until all batter is used. Stack wrappers on a plate.

Prepare the Sauce:

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, soy sauce, and sugar. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add garlic and diluted cornstarch. Stir until the sauce thickens. Set aside.

Assemble the Lumpiang Ubod:

  1. Place a wrapper on a plate. Add a spoonful of filling near one end. Fold the sides and roll tightly.
  2. Drizzle the sauce over the roll before serving.

Enjoy your Lumpiang Ubod with a garnish of crushed peanuts or garlic for extra flavor!
