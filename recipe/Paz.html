La Paz Batchoy Recipe

Make a La Paz Batchoy in simple step!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork (preferably pork belly or ribs)
  • 1/2 cup shrimp or fish stock
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicharrón (crispy pork skin), crushed
  • 1/2 cup pork liver, chopped (optional)
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 200 grams miki noodles (egg noodles)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pork in water for 30 minutes or until tender. Set aside the meat and broth.
  2. In a pot, sauté garlic and onions, then add the pork, stock, soy sauce, and beef broth.
  3. Bring to a boil, and then add the miki noodles.
  4. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the noodles are cooked and the broth has thickened slightly.
  5. Add pork liver and chicharrón. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious La Paz Batchoy!
