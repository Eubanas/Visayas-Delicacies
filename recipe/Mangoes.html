Dried MangoesRecipe

Make a Dried Mangoes in simple step!

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe mangoes (preferably Carabao variety)
  • Sugar (optional, for sweetening)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Peel the mangoes and remove the pit.
  2. Slice the mangoes thinly.
  3. Place the mango slices on a baking sheet and sprinkle a little sugar and salt (optional) to enhance flavor.
  4. Dry the mango slices in a dehydrator or in an oven at a low temperature (about 135°F or 57°C) for 6-8 hours, or until they are leathery and pliable.
  5. Allow them to cool and store in an airtight container.

Enjoy your delicious Dried Mangoes!
