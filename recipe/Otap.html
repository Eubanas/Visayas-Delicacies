Otap Recipe

Make a Otap in various step!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine or butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add butter and vanilla, and mix until it forms a dough.
  3. Roll the dough out thinly and cut into rectangles or your desired shape.
  4. Brush with egg wash (beaten egg) on top.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 15 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Allow the otap to cool and dust with powdered sugar before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Otap!
