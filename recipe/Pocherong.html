Pocherong Bisaya Recipe

Make a Pocherong Bisaya in various step!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork (shoulder or belly), sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 head of cabbage, chopped
  • 2 liters water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© the garlic, onion, and tomato until softened.
  2. Add the pork slices and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in water and bring it to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes until the pork is tender.
  4. Add the potatoes and carrots, and cook until soft.
  5. Add the cabbage, season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce, and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Pocherong Bisaya!
