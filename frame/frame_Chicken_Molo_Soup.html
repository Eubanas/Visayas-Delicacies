Chicken Molo Soup

Chicken Molo Soup, also known as Pancit Molo, originates from the Molo district of Iloilo City in the Philippines. The dish is an adaptation of Chinese wonton soup, introduced by early Chinese immigrants in the area before Spanish colonization. Over time, it was localized with Filipino flavors and ingredients, becoming a celebrated comfort food.
